Anchoring: 25 Years of PCE

Hosted the 25th-anniversary celebration of Pillai College of Engineering, managing the stage and engaging the audience throughout the event.

Event Info

  • My Role: Anchor / Event Host
  • Category: Public Speaking / Event Management
  • Core Skills: Public Speaking, Stage Presence, Audience Engagement

Event Overview

As the master of ceremonies for the 25th-anniversary celebration of Pillai College of Engineering (PCE), I had the honor of guiding an audience of over 500 students, faculty, and distinguished guests through a memorable event. My role was to ensure the program flowed seamlessly while keeping the energy high and the audience engaged from start to finish.

Key Responsibilities

  • Commanded the stage, setting a professional and engaging tone for the entire celebration.
  • Introduced guest speakers, performers, and award recipients with well-researched and personalized remarks.
  • Maintained the event's schedule, smoothly transitioning between different segments of the program.
  • Interacted with the audience, using humor and charisma to create a lively and inclusive atmosphere.
  • Demonstrated strong stage presence and clear communication, ensuring all announcements and proceedings were understood by everyone.
