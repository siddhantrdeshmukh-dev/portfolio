Project Info

Project Overview

As a core member of the administrative and business strategy team for Spark Racing, I was instrumental in the non-engineering aspects of our Formula Student competition entry. This role required a blend of strategic planning, financial management, and persuasive communication to ensure the team was well-funded and organized.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed and authored the official Business Plan Presentation (BPP) for the competition, outlining our team's market strategy, financial projections, and operational plans.
  • Led sponsorship acquisition efforts, identifying potential corporate partners and pitching the team's value proposition to secure funding and in-kind support.
  • Managed team logistics, including vendor coordination for parts and travel arrangements for competition events.
  • Presented the business plan to judges and potential sponsors, honing public speaking and presentation skills in a high-pressure environment.
