Tesla Coil

A hands-on electronics project to build a resonant transformer circuit for demonstrating high-voltage, wireless power transmission.

Project Info

  • My Role: Project Lead / Designer
  • Category: Personal Electronics Project
  • Core Skills: Electronics, Circuit Design, High-Voltage Safety
  • Key Achievement: Successful Wireless Power Demo

Project Overview

This project was a deep dive into the principles of electromagnetism and resonant energy transfer. The goal was to design, source, and construct a functional spark gap Tesla coil capable of wirelessly illuminating fluorescent bulbs from a distance, showcasing the fascinating physics behind Nikola Tesla's invention.

Key Responsibilities

  • Researched and calculated the required specifications for the primary and secondary coils, capacitor bank, and spark gap to achieve resonance.
  • Sourced all high-voltage components, including a neon-sign transformer, high-voltage capacitors, and copper wiring.
  • Carefully constructed the circuit, with a strong emphasis on high-voltage safety protocols, proper grounding, and insulation.
  • Assembled and wound the coils, built the spark gap, and integrated all components into a stable, functional unit.
  • Successfully tested and demonstrated the coil's ability to generate a high-frequency corona discharge and wirelessly power nearby fluorescent tubes.
