Project Overview

In my role as a Business Operations Associate, I acted as the crucial link between the central Chotu Maharaj franchise team and local deployment teams. The project focused on establishing new cinema locations while strictly adhering to the parent company's brand and operational guidelines.

Key Responsibilities

  • Facilitated clear and consistent communication to align local cinema setups with the core franchise's standards and brand identity.
  • Acted as the primary point of contact for local teams, addressing operational queries, resolving issues, and ensuring compliance.
  • Provided regular, detailed updates to the franchise headquarters, ensuring transparency and efficient project tracking.
  • Helped streamline the setup process by creating checklists and communication protocols to improve efficiency and reduce setup time.
