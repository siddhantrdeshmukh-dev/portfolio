Body Naturals

A business concept for a new brand of organic skincare products, from market research to brand identity.

Project Info

  • My Role: Founder / Brand Strategist
  • Category: Business Concept / Entrepreneurship
  • Core Skills: Market Research, Brand Development, Product Strategy
  • Key Achievement: Comprehensive Go-to-Market Plan

Project Overview

Body Naturals was a conceptual project to develop a complete business plan for a direct-to-consumer organic skincare brand. The core idea was to create a line of products using ethically sourced, natural ingredients, presented in sustainable packaging, and marketed to a health-conscious, millennial audience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conducted in-depth market research to identify target demographics, analyze competitors, and find a unique niche in the crowded skincare market.
  • Defined the brand's mission, vision, and unique selling proposition, focusing on transparency and sustainability.
  • Developed a hypothetical product line strategy, including key ingredients, product formulations, and pricing structure.
  • Created a comprehensive go-to-market plan that covered branding, packaging design, e-commerce strategy, and a digital marketing launch campaign.
